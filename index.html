About

ResearchSpace connects information about people, places, periods, things and concepts from different sources without losing their original context, and allows the community to enrich information as a collaborative activity. It structures information as networks of knowledge supporting both a linear and non-linear approach to historical questions and encouraging different perspectives.

ResearchSpace is open source, open standards, customisable and extensible, designed to be used with a wide range of knowledge based projects and applications.
